Our Mission

Empowering Citizens Through Transparency

Good governance is the foundation of a thriving society. BR27 is dedicated to bringing transparency and understanding to government policies, citizen rights, and civic engagement opportunities. We simplify complex policies and regulations, helping people understand how they can actively participate in shaping their communities and holding their representatives accountable.

A simple form you can fill to ask the government any question about how they spend public money or make decisions. They must reply within 30 days.

Gram Sabha

"Village-level democratic assembly under Panchayati Raj..."

A meeting where all adults in your village can speak up, vote on local issues, and decide how to spend village funds. Your voice matters here.

A legal request to the court asking them to protect your fundamental rights when the government or someone violates them. Like a complaint to the highest authority.

Take Action

How You Can Participate

Simple, practical steps to become an active participant in governance.

1

Stay Informed

Read government notifications, attend Gram Sabha meetings, follow official channels for policy updates.

2

Exercise Your Vote

Register to vote, research candidates, participate in local and national elections to choose your representatives.

3

Engage Locally

Attend ward meetings, join resident associations, participate in public consultations on local development.

4

Hold Accountable

Use RTI to access information, file grievances through proper channels, demand transparency from officials.
