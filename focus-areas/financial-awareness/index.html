Your Path

Financial Independence Journey

Step-by-step roadmap from financial chaos to complete control.

1

Track & Budget

Write down every rupee you earn and spend for 1 month. See where money goes. Cut unnecessary expenses. Follow 50-30-20 rule: needs, wants, savings.

2

Build Emergency Fund

Save 6 months of expenses in a savings account or liquid fund. This protects you from job loss, medical emergencies, unexpected repairs.

3

Clear Bad Debt & Get Insurance

Pay off credit cards and personal loans first. Get term insurance (‚Çπ1 crore cover costs ‚Çπ500/month) and health insurance for family protection.

4

Start Investing Consistently
