Your Path

Career Planning Journey

Step-by-step roadmap from student to successful professional.

1

Self-Assessment (Class 9-10)

Identify interests, strengths, and values. Take aptitude tests. Talk to professionals in different fields. Don't rush‚Äîexplore multiple options before deciding your stream.

2

Stream & Preparation (Class 11-12)

Focus on chosen stream. Prepare for entrance exams if needed. Research colleges, courses, and career paths. Build foundational skills and maintain good grades.

3

College & Skill Building (Graduation)

Excel academically but also do internships, projects, certifications. Build network, join clubs, develop soft skills. Learn beyond textbooks‚Äîonline courses, workshops.

4

Career Launch & Growth

Land first job or pursue higher studies. Continuously upskill, network, and seek mentorship. Stay adaptable‚Äîyour first career choice isn't forever. Keep learning and growing.
